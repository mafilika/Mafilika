Pricing & Investment

How much does a website cost?

Pricing depends on scope — a landing page, a full corporate site and a custom e-commerce platform all carry different investment levels. We provide a fixed, transparent quote after understanding your goals during your free strategy session.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. Most projects are structured as a deposit followed by milestone payments, and ongoing services are billed monthly.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. Every quote outlines exactly what is included — design, development, hosting, SEO and support — before any work begins.

What is included in a monthly retainer?

Retainers typically include hosting, maintenance, SEO, content updates and reporting. Exact inclusions are agreed upfront and detailed in your proposal.

Do you offer a money-back guarantee?

Where applicable to a specific package, guarantee terms are outlined in your proposal before you sign, so there are no surprises.

Timelines & Process

How long does it take to build a website?

Most business websites launch within 2–4 weeks. Larger corporate or e-commerce builds may take 4–8 weeks depending on complexity.

How long does an AI automation project take?

A chatbot or booking automation can typically be live within 1–3 weeks once your processes and integrations are mapped out.

What do you need from me to get started?

Your logo and brand assets (if you have them), a short brief on your goals, and access to any existing accounts we'll be working within.

Will I be able to see progress during the build?

Yes, you'll receive staged previews and check-ins at each phase of the process — Design, Develop and pre-Launch.

Websites

Can you redesign my existing website?

Yes. We can rebuild your current site from scratch while preserving your rankings, content and brand recognition where valuable.

Do you offer hosting?

Yes, we offer secure, fast, managed hosting as part of most packages, including SSL, backups and uptime monitoring.

Will my website work on mobile?

Every website we build is fully responsive and tested across mobile, tablet and desktop before launch.

Can I update the website myself afterwards?

Yes, we build on editor-friendly platforms and can train you and your team, or we can manage updates for you.

Do you build online stores?

Yes, we design and develop full e-commerce websites with secure payments, inventory and order management.

SEO & AI Search

Will my website rank on Google?

We build every site with SEO best practice from day one and run ongoing local and technical SEO, though ranking timelines vary by industry and competition.

Do you manage SEO on an ongoing basis?

Yes, ongoing SEO is available as a monthly service covering technical health, content, local listings and reporting.

What is AI search optimisation?

It's the process of structuring your content and data so that AI tools such as ChatGPT and Gemini can find, understand and recommend your business.

How long does SEO take to show results?

Local SEO improvements are often visible within 60–90 days, while competitive national rankings can take longer.

AI & Automation

Do you build AI chatbots?

Yes, we design and deploy AI chatbots for websites and WhatsApp that answer questions, qualify leads and book appointments.

Can automation replace my receptionist?

It won't replace the human touch entirely, but it will handle repetitive enquiries and bookings so your team can focus on higher-value conversations.

Is WhatsApp automation reliable?

Yes, we build on official, compliant WhatsApp Business integrations designed for consistent, reliable delivery.

Can you connect automation to my CRM?

Yes, we integrate with most common CRMs, or can set one up for you if you don't already have one.

Support & Ongoing Partnership

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, every client has access to a South African support team for updates, questions and troubleshooting.

What happens if something breaks on my site?

Support clients get priority fixes, typically resolved within one business day depending on severity.

Can we scale up services later?

Yes, our packages are designed to grow with you — from a single website to a full marketing and automation system.
