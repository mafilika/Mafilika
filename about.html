About Mafilika Holdings & Innovation

Our Story

Why we started Mafilika Holdings & Innovation

Too many good South African businesses lose contracts and customers to competitors with weaker offerings but stronger digital presence. We started Mafilika Holdings & Innovation to close that gap — building enterprise-grade websites, AI automation and marketing systems that make any business look, rank and convert like a market leader.

Based in Christiana in the North West, we work with construction firms, security companies, restaurants, hotels, supermarkets, manufacturers, property developers, schools, medical practices, logistics companies and professional service businesses across South Africa.

We measure our success the same way you do: leads generated, tenders won, tables booked, and customers retained.
